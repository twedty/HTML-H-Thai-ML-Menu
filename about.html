Why did this one place become That One Place?

Idiotville, Oregon, USA

We know that when someone is drunk, incapacitated, or otherwise not in the right mind, that it may be quite hard to remember a name to a place that one may want to eat at. So, what better way than to have a name that sticks out? In Idiotville, Oregon we figured that the best way to retain our roots was to make a name that resembled our town as well. From here, we've spread out to many different and unique locations that completely coincide and work with our name as a whole.
